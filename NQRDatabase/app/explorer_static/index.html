NQR Database Overview

This database combines nitrogen, chlorine, potassium, and related NQR measurements from archived CWRU/University of Florida pages, Navy/NRL line tables, King's College experimental notes, and reviewed Landolt-Bornstein table excerpts.

Records

Compounds contain one or more samples. Samples represent a reported measurement condition, often including method and temperature.

Sites

Sites hold isotope assignments and coupling parameters. The quadrupole coupling constant is displayed as CQ; asymmetry is η.

Lines

Lines are observed transition frequencies. Relaxation times, linewidths, temperature coefficients, forms, and source temperatures are shown when recorded.

Landolt Assumption

Landolt frequency lists and CQ/η pairs are not automatically assigned to each other unless the reviewed source makes that assignment explicit.
